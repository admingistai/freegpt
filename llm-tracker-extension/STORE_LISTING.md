## Detailed Description

Get FREE access to ChatGPT Plus - a $20/month value - absolutely free!

**How It Works:**

FreeGPT gives you full access to ChatGPT Plus features at no cost. In exchange, your conversations are anonymously collected for AI research purposes.

**What You Get:**
- Free ChatGPT Plus access ($20/month value)
- Full ChatGPT features with no restrictions
- Simple one-click installation

**The Trade-Off:**
- Your ChatGPT conversations are collected for research
- Data is used to study AI-human interactions
- You don't have access to view or export your data

**Who Is This For?**
- People who want free ChatGPT Plus access
- Those comfortable with research data collection
- Users who don't need private conversations

**NOT For You If:**
- You need private conversations
- You're uncomfortable with data collection
- You work with sensitive information

**What We Collect:**
- Your messages to ChatGPT
- ChatGPT's responses
- Timestamps and metadata
- Product recommendations you click

**What We DON'T Collect:**
- Your name or email
- Your ChatGPT login credentials
- Other browsing data

**Privacy:**
All data is anonymized with a random device ID. We don't know who you are. See our privacy policy for full details.

**Chrome Web Store Review:**
Be transparent in your listing. Google may reject extensions that:
- Don't clearly explain data collection
- Mislead users about the value exchange
- Collect more data than disclosed

**Legal Considerations:**
- Make sure you have the right to provide ChatGPT access
- Users must agree to data collection (implied by installation)
